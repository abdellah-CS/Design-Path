Motion Designer

Step by step path to becoming a modern motion designer in 2023

1- Fundamentals of Animation

Study Animation Principles
  • Understand the core principles of animation, such as timing, spacing, squash and stretch, anticipation, and follow-through
Learn About Keyframes
  • Familiarize yourself with keyframes as the foundation of animation
Motion Graphics Basics
  • Explore the basics of motion graphics, including text animations and simple transitions

2- Animation Software

Choose Animation Software
  • Select animation software that suits your needs, such as Adobe After Effects, Adobe Animate, or other animation tools
Software Proficiency
  • Learn the basics of your chosen animation software, including creating compositions, layers, and keyframes

3- Graphic Design Skills

Design Fundamentals
  • Build a strong foundation in graphic design principles, including layout, typography, and color theory
Illustrator and Photoshop
  • Familiarize yourself with Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop, as they are often used for creating assets in motion design

4- Storytelling and Scripting

Narrative Structure
  • Understand storytelling and how it applies to motion design projects
Scripting
  • Learn to write scripts and storyboards to plan your animations

5- Motion Graphics and Typography

Typography in Motion
  • Explore kinetic typography and text animation techniques
Motion Graphics Projects
  • Create motion graphics projects, including logo animations, lower thirds, and infographic animations

6- Character Animation

Character Design
  • Learn about designing for accessibility and compliance with WCAG guidelinesLearn character design principles for animations
Rigging and Animation
  • Study character rigging and animation techniques for characters and creatures

7- 2D and 3D Animation

2D Animation
  • Master 2D animation techniques, including frame-by-frame animation
3D Animation
  • Explore 3D animation software and techniques if you're interested in 3D motion design

8- Visual Effects (VFX)

Special Effects
  • Learn to create visual effects like explosions, fire, and particles
Compositing
  • Understand compositing techniques for integrating VFX into live-action footage

9- Advanced Techniques

Advanced Animation
  • Explore advanced animation techniques such as character rigging, expressions, and simulations
Scripting and Expressions
  • Learn scripting and expressions to automate tasks and create complex animations

10- Portfolio Building

Create a Diverse Portfolio
  • Showcase a range of motion design projects that demonstrate your skills and creativity

11- Professional Development

Networking
  • Join motion design communities, attend industry events, and connect with other professionals
Freelancing or Employment
  • Decide if you want to work as a freelance motion designer or seek employment at animation studios, advertising agencies, or other creative companies

12- Continuous Learning

Stay Updated
  • Keep up with the latest trends and techniques in motion design
Online Courses and Tutorials
  • Enroll in advanced motion design courses and watch tutorials to expand your knowledge
Mentorship
  • Consider finding a mentor in the field to provide guidance and feedback
You are now a Motion Designer!
